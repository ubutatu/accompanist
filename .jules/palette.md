## 2025-01-24 - Visual Success Indicators in Permissions
**Learning:** Adding a visual success indicator (like a checkmark icon) alongside text feedback provides immediate confirmation to the user and improves the overall polish of the UI.
**Action:** Always pair status text with a corresponding icon (e.g., `Icons.Default.Done` for success) in `Row` layouts with `Arrangement.spacedBy(8.dp)` to enhance visual communication.
